## 🎯 **HOW TO USE**

### **1. Import the Theme**
The theme is already imported in `main.css`. Just ensure it stays first.

### **2. Replace Layout Components**
```vue
<template>
    <DigitalTwinLayout>
        <router-view />
    </DigitalTwinLayout>
</template>
```

### **3. Use Enhanced Components**
```vue
<EnhancedKpiCard
    title="Total Revenue"
    value="2.4M"
    unit="FCFA"
    icon="RevenueIcon"
    variant="success"
    :change="18.5"
    :chart-data="chartData"
/>
```

### **4. Apply to Existing Views**
Replace existing views with their `_Enhanced.vue` counterparts for the full Digital Twin experience.
